My journey to becoming a developer began a long time ago in 6th grade. We had a computer class where we played games and learded typing, but we also had the opportunity to play with HTML. Fast forward a decade and some change, where I found myself feeling unfufilled. I'd spend hours on the internet after coming home from work, wasting time until I would go to bed and start it all over again. I wasnt feeling like my creativity was being put to use, so I found a few links to some free online classes to re-learn some HTML and try out some CSS. After the first couple of classes, I was hooked. From then on, it has been my goal to become a developer.
